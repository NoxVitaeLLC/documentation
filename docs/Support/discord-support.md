If you need help or technical assistance, you can contact Nox Vitae staff through Discord.

**Recommended approach:**
- Open a ticket in our [Nox Vitae's Discord](https://discord.gg/NoxVitae) -(we have a ticket dedicated to Bot support, spam/scammer reports, and general help tickets)
- Provide your username and any relevant screenshots
- Describe the issue clearly
- Clearly advise if this evidence is coming from you, or a 2nd/3rd party.
