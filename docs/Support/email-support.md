# Support via Email

You can reach the Nox Vitae support team directly via:

📧 **support@noxvitae.com**  

---

## 📍 Important Notes

> **Nox Vitae staff are not legal authorities or platform officials.**  
We provide community and procedural guidance only. We do **not** act as representatives of platforms such as **VRChat**, **Discord**, **Patreon**, or any other external services.

---

## 🔍 If Your Issue Occurs on Another Platform

If your concern involves an incident that took place outside of Nox Vitae-managed environments (e.g. within VRChat public instances, third-party Discord servers, or social platforms):

- ➡️ We will provide **guidance and links** to the appropriate reporting channels  
- ➡️ **We cannot/do not collect evidence** against individuals or organizations for public use.  
- ➡️ **We cannot escalate or enforce anything outside our administration scope**



---

## 📎 Example Resolution Flow

| Scenario | Our Role |
|----------|----------|
| Violation *within* Nox Vitae’s public Discord (e.g. `discord.gg/NoxVitae`) | Investigate internally and take appropriate action. We may escalate to Discord Trust & Safety depending on severity. |
| Behavior occurring in another Discord server | Recommend contacting that server’s leadership and submitting a report to Discord Trust & Safety if appropriate. |
| Incident occurring in VRChat (outside of a Nox Vitae-managed event) | Provide guidance on how to submit a VRChat Trust & Safety report. For safety concerns, we strongly encourage contacting local authorities. |
| Illegal or high-risk behavior reported via third-party sources | We will advise submitting reports directly to appropriate platform safety teams and, if necessary, encourage contacting local law enforcement. We cannot act without first-party evidence. |

## 🚨 When to Contact Authorities or Platform Safety Teams

If your report may involve:
- Interaction with or targeting of a **minor**
- **Harassment**, stalking, or threats
- **Sexual misconduct**
- **Illegal activity** or coercion

➡️ You should file reports directly to the platform where the safety concern happens. Please do not forget to report the issues directly to your local law enforcement. 

Our team can **help recommend the best authrity to contact** but **we cannot file on your/another's behalf**.

---

## 📮 Response Timeline

We aim to respond to emails within **3–5 business days**. For high-severity incidents, please select the correct **Urgent** flag in your message and provide:

✔ Detailed description of the issue  
✔ Username(s) involved (VRC, Discord, etc.)  
✔ Date/time and platform  
✔ Evidence if available (screenshots, clip links)
✔ Confirmation on source of evidence (example: if it's your own evidence, please state so. If this evidence comes from another person or source, please denote that)

---

## 🤝 Our Commitment

We will always:
- Treat your report with respect and discretion
- Provide guidance based on our policies
- Maintain alignment with our **Privacy Policy** & **Data Handling Procedures**

We **will not**:
- Take action outside our administrative control scope
- Override platform moderation decisions
- Act as legal representatives
