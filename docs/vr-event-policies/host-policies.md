# Host Policies

Hosts are responsible for ensuring a fun, safe, and engaging environment for all attendees while supporting event operations.

---

## Responsibilities

- If needed, assist with hosting card games or other planned activities, ensuring a smooth experience.
- Walk new members through the world and show them how to use available features.
- Answer questions and provide guidance to attendees regarding event activities, rules, and locations.

---

## Crowd Management & Assistance

- Assist in crowd control, preventing clustering or overwhelming of areas.
- Direct attendees when relocating to different areas within the event space.

---

## Professional Conduct

:::warning
You are a representative of Nox Vitae. Absolutely NO drama while you are a host for an event. You will be removed if you are discussing other club drama or attendees.
:::
