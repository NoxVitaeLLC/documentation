# Security Policies

Security staff are responsible for maintaining a safe, rule-abiding environment throughout all Nox Vitae events.

:::note
Security staff have moderation permissions.
:::

---

## Core Responsibilities

- Monitor the instance for disruptive behavior including trolling, harassment, and excessive spam, and enforce community rules.
- Identify and address conflicts or escalating situations in a calm and professional manner.
- Be on the lookout (BOLO) for obvious minors attempting to bypass the 18+ restriction.
- Report and remove problematic individuals if necessary, following moderation protocols.
- Ensure all event guidelines are followed by both attendees and collaborators.

---

## Crowd Management & Assistance

- Assist in crowd control, preventing clustering or overwhelming of areas.
- Direct attendees when relocating to different areas within the event space.
- Answer questions and provide guidance to attendees regarding event activities, rules, and locations.

---

## Professional Conduct

:::warning
You are a representative of Nox Vitae. Absolutely NO drama while you are on security for an event. You will be removed if you are discussing other club drama or attendees.
:::
